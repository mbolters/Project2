About

Star Scout is a site created for the purpose of discovering upcoming astrological events, where the least light pollution is, and the best local camping areas for viewing. You can view our general calendar of events or select specific events from the home page carousel. You won't forget your plans because once you've chosen a campsite you can save the event to your google calendar!
